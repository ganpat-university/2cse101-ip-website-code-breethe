Breethe: The Art of Letting Go

What is Breethe?

BREETHE is a platform that identifies the notion of mental health as a very essential portion of normal human existence. Breethe: The Art of Letting Go brings you a forum where you can anonymously open your heart, discuss all that you have stored up inside yourself. Breethe brings you the opportunnity to discover and enlighten others about some of the most underestimated and underrated issues that can affect the mental well being of a person, in general.

Why Choose Breethe?

Breethe is a developing platform where activists, psychologists, mental well being professionals, common people (people facing and fighting issues) can interact and work for a mutual therapy. Survey Section would be soon launched for general and specific medical surveys to keep a record and graph the mental illness pattern and type growth ratio. basically, to track the mental illness growth.

How to Become a Part of Breethe?

To connect with breethe and join the family, you are needed to fill up a form and sign up. The information would be a confidential entity, accessed only by us for membership and therapeutical purposes. The social media handles can be refered for the daily activities and events.
